DRESS CODE

  1. Students should wear their uniforms while going to college.
  2. On other days they have to wear long top and bottom with a scarf.
  3. Students have to wear decent pyjamas while going to mess with a scarf.
  4. Short tops and pants are strictly not allowed.

Hostel rules

  1. Students are allowed to use mobile phones only from 7 p.m. to 9 p.m.
  2. Students are not allowed to use any kind of electronic devices like iron box,heater etc.
  3. Students should get their leave form signed by the warden one day before they are going home
  4. Students should give their evening attendance before 5:30 without fail
  5. Students should inform the warden and take permission from her before going anywhere on non working days.

Mess rules

  1. Students should not waste food.
  2. They are requested to take as much food as required and complete it without wasting.
  3. Students are not allowed to carry food along with them to the hostel.
  4. Students who are sick can get food from the mess only if they seek permission from their respeted warden through a letter and get it signed.
  5. Non-veg is strictly not allowed in the mess hall.

Prayer rules

During the prayer time when warden rings the bell all the students are requested to assemble in the prayer hall and requested to pray or meditate to god for half-hour.

Cleanliness

All the stdents are required to clean their rooms by themselves and keep it neat and hygenic.Students have to wash their own clothes. In case if any student falls sick and cannot wash their clothes in that case warden will arrange a dhobi and You have to pay for that person.
